Technological Intervention to Improve Mental Health Literacy, Anxiety, and Depression among Youths in Nigeria (TINMAN)

Thank you for your interest in assessing the state of your emotional wellbeing and your knowledge about mental health. Your responses to this survey are voluntary and should not take more than 15 to 20 minutes of your time.

We will not be requesting your name or any other identifying information. Thus, it will be anonymous, and your confidentiality is guaranteed. The information you provide will help you gain a better understanding of your emotional wellbeing and help us learn more about it too.

Thank you for your time and contributions to the general emotional wellbeing of our society.
